How it works
WoE → Logistic Regression

Each input is mapped to a Weight of Evidence value, encoding its default rate relative to the portfolio average. These WoE values are combined in a logistic regression to produce the log-odds of default, which is converted to a probability via the logistic function.

Score Scaling (PDO=20)

The log-odds are converted to a scorecard using the PDO (Points to Double the Odds) method. PDO=20 means every 20-point drop in score doubles the odds of default. Base Score=600, Base Odds=1. Score range: 300–850.

IFRS 9 Staging & ECL

Stage 1 (PD <1%): 12-month ECL. Stage 2 (1%–20%): lifetime ECL required due to significant increase in credit risk (SICR). Stage 3 (PD >20% or 3+ delinquencies): credit-impaired, full lifetime ECL. ECL = PD × LGD × EAD.
