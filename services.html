Services

How I can help you

Beyond full-time roles, I take on short-term engagements where I can add genuine value. Whether you need a credit risk model built, an IFRS 9 framework designed, or guidance navigating a finance qualification — I am open to a conversation.

For Professionals

Need a credit risk model built from scratch?

End-to-end PD, LGD, and EAD frameworks in Python — from raw data through WoE/IV feature engineering, scorecard development, and full validation. Aligned with IFRS 9 and Basel III/IV.

Want your existing model independently reviewed?

Independent validation covering discriminatory power (AUROC, Gini, KS), calibration, population stability, and regulatory alignment — delivered as a written findings report.

Building an IFRS 9 ECL framework?

Help design or review staging criteria, lifetime PD estimation, macro-economic overlays, and ECL calculation methodology for your retail or corporate portfolio.

For Students

Struggling with CFA Level I or II?

Having passed CFA Level II, I help you build a study plan, work through quantitative methods, fixed income, and derivatives, and develop the exam discipline that actually works.

Applying for an MSc in Finance or Banking?

I completed my MSc Banking & International Finance at Bayes Business School, City, University of London. School selection, personal statement, and interview preparation.

Want to break into credit risk analytics?

What hiring managers look for, how to build a relevant Python project portfolio, and how to position yourself for quantitative risk roles.
